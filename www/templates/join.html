Joining

To join ricebowl, talk to a few people in #rice@irc.rizon.net so that we get to know you. When that is done, you can ask an admin to add you to the contest.
You will then get a unique, secret key that you can use to submit your screenshot and vote. You can ask ricebot for your key at any time with the getkey command.

Current admins are: eti, nai, icyphox.
