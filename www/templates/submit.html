Submitting

When the next tournament begins, you can submit your screenshot by making a POST request to this page. For example, using curl: 

curl -F "file=@screenshot.png&key=<your_key>" http://rice.church/submit
or httpie: 
httpie --form "http://rice.church/submit" file@screenshot.png key==<your_key>
